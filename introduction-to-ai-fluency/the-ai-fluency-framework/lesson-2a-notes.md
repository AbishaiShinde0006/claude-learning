The 3 Modes of Interacting with AI

Automation — The AI performs tasks based on the information or instructions you give it. Used when you know exactly what you want to achieve and have a clear vision.
Augmentation — You and the AI work together. The AI assists you wherever you're stuck. Used when you don't know the exact final product. It's a collaborative partnership.
Agency — You give the AI a goal and it figures out the steps and executes them independently, making decisions along the way without you guiding each step.

The 4 D's Framework

Diligence — Taking responsibility for the way you use AI, being transparent about the extent to which AI is used, and using it responsibly.
Description — Telling the AI exactly what to do. Involves meaningful conversations. You must know what has to be accomplished and give detailed instructions including audience, format, tone, and constraints.
Delegation — Analysing what tasks should be completed by humans and which by AI, then allocating work accordingly. Requires knowing the strengths and capabilities of AI.
Discernment — After AI performs a task, analyse whether it actually helped you reach your goal. Always verify and personally assess important outputs.
