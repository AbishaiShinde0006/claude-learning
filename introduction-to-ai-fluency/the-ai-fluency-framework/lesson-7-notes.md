Focused on 6 tips for achieving a good quality prompt

1. Give context: Tell the AI who you are, what output you expect and how you want the AI to approach the problem

2. Give examples: Give sample examples to the AI of the output that you expect. 
This helps when you need the output in a certain format or a certain way and it is difficult to explain, giving examples is more helpful

3.  Defining output constraints: Means specifying format, length, tone, structure. 

4. Assigning a role: Like "think like a structural engineer" is called role prompting.

5. Allow the AI to think: AI needs space to think for a better response. 
Some AI models are not design to think but most of them are but still you can tell the AI to take the time to think properly so that it can give you the better output

6. Explain difficult topics in simple words breakdown complex tasks into simple steps

Bonus: asking AI to help you improve your own prompt.
For eg. "Here is my prompt. How can I improve it to get a better response?"
