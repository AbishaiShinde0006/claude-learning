Capabilities:

Works in different languages
Compensates for human limitations
Can connect to APIs to automate work
Capable of performing multiple tasks
Increases scalability and speed
Recognises patterns

Limitations:

Struggles with complex mathematical and logical problems
Has a context window — if too much information is given, older info gets pushed out and forgotten
Knowledge cutoff — trained only up to a certain date, unaware of anything beyond that
Hallucinations — answers confidently even when the answer is completely wrong
Inconsistent outputs — ask the same question twice and you may get two different answers
