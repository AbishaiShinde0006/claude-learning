Product Description — defining what outcome you want the AI to achieve. 
Don't just keep giving prompts, instead have a two way discussion and make the AI think by: 
giving it context, pushing back, and treating it like a conversation not a search engine.

Process Description — defining the way you want the AI to think. 
Give it information related to your specific task and problem. 
Even though AI has been trained on a lot of data, your case is different — give it the context it needs.

Performance Description — telling the AI how you want the output. 
Specify the format, the type of data you want considered, and any constraints on how the output should be structured.
